alpha Kappa Delta Phi Sorority at VCU Recruitment Season
As Webmistress/ Public Relations Chair of alpha Kappa Delta Phi at VCU in Fall 2022, Spring 2023, and Spring 2024, I was tasked to make a lot of content to advertise our sorority out to members of the VCU community, particualrly for our recruitment period. These informations flyers are created on theme, with the theme being decided by an executive board member. They follow our sorority brand guidelines, along with dislaying all information for our events on the back.
Year:  2022, 2023, 2024


Skills:  Adobe Illustrator, Procreate, Social Media
