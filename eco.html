What's in Your Ecosystem?
An interactive installation of community ecosystem
This collaborative installation was done with Nadia Ali, Amelia Magee, and Gigi Pham-Ton. Prompted to create a community ecosystem, we decided to create a space for those inside our community at VCU Arts to contribute to our ecosystem. We left educational and instructional zines to provide context and directions, along with creating a poster with directions for passerbys to contribute. We provided pens, scissors, string, and cut out paper shapes in little vellum pockets on the wall, and we started our own ecosystem of connections before leaving it to the rest of the community. Each string is a connection, and different shapes meant different themes in an ecosystem.
Year:  2022


Skills:  Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, Print Production
