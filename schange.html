Sustainable Change
Collaborative resource book created for the Richmond community
Working with Nadia Ali, Amelia Magee, and Gigi Pham-Ton, we curated a collection of sustinable resources for food, textiles, and managing waste. Printed on a risograph printer using soy ink, on recyled paper, this book is sustainble inside and out. We created a branding guide with fonts, colors, and general sizes together, but split ways to each create 4-5 different spreads to go inside the book. The content contains metaphors and connections to stages of life, along with research-supported sustinable methods that aim to inform the general public.
