STRLIT
Starlit, a curated shoe branding project
We were given a shoe at random, and had to create a visual identity for that shoe. I was given an American sex worker shoe from 1995, that had "aggressive" embellishment and strapping suggestive of bondage wear. It is a shoe that inspired mainstream fashion footwear design, and I chose to utilize tape as a metaphor for flashiness yet fragility. Tape attaches objects back together but has a reflective quality on its own.

We had to produce a poster, a profile picture, a video, and a gif.
Year:  2023
