Pigment Pianist
A website archive collection of sounds turned into colors
Visit my site: https://kali-tan.github.io/pigmentpianist/

The website, Pigment Pianist, stands as a translation machine. It takes sound from the user's environment through the microphone, translating it into decibels and hertz. Those decible and hertz are then translated into a hexadecimal code, with the decibles correlating to red and green and the hertz correlating to blue. This hexcode can then be saved in a local archive, and my own collection of over 105+ colors is available to view as well, with information about how it was collected, the time it was collected, and the hexcode.
